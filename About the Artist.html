Maher Zain is an islamic Swedish songwriter. He was born july 16 1981. His parents were Lebenese but they emigrated to Swedin when he was only 8 years old. While he was there, he finished school and got his bacholers degree. in 2005 he entered the music industry.

In 2009, he teamed up with Awakening records and produced his first official album which was titled "Thank you Allah" and containes 13 songs total.

Later on in his life he made 2 more successful albums; in 2012 he made one titled "Forgive Me" and it contained 14 songs. In 2016 he made another one titled "One" which has 15 songs.

Although most of his songs are in English, he has many that are in other languages such as Aribic, Turkish, and French.

Through out his musical career he's fetured lot's of other artist in his songs, and he continues to make great songs for Awakening records.
